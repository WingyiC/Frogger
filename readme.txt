Game: Frogger
How to play?:

Press start to start the game. Use up, down, left, right keys to control the frogs to cross the road and the river. Avoid the cars, the river, and the mouth of the crocodiles. The trucks and the woods are safe. You have 3 lives and to win this game, you will need to navigate 5 frogs across the river and road. Enjoy.
